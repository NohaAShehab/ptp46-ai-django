About ITI

Information Technology Institute — Empowering Egypt's next generation of tech professionals since 1993.

Who We Are

The Information Technology Institute (ITI) is a leading national institute affiliated with the Egyptian Ministry of Communications and Information Technology. Since its establishment in 1993, ITI has been dedicated to building highly skilled ICT professionals through intensive, world-class training programs that bridge the gap between academia and the technology industry.
